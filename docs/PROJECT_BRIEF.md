# Woosh Website — Project Brief

## What is Woosh?

Woosh is a premium doorstep vehicle care startup offering wash and care services for cars, bikes, autos, and subscription-based plans. The brand promise is **Clean. Fast. Smart.** with professional service delivered at the customer's location.

## Target Users

- Vehicle owners who want premium cleaning without visiting a wash center
- Busy professionals seeking convenient doorstep service
- Monthly plan subscribers looking for consistent care and value
- Daily commuters needing quick interior freshness

## Business Goal

Build trust, drive bookings, and establish Woosh as a premium, reliable vehicle care brand ahead of full app and CRM rollout.

## Website Goal

Create a conversion-focused marketing website that:

- Communicates premium quality and trust
- Showcases services with real imagery and video
- Captures interest via login/signup and booking CTAs
- Provides a polished foundation for future backend integration

## Current Scope (Frontend Only)

This v1 build includes:

- Landing page with all core marketing sections
- Service detail placeholder pages
- Login/signup modal (UI only)
- Documentation and design system

It does **not** include backend APIs, real authentication, booking flows, payments, CRM, or admin dashboards.
